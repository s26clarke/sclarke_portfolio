OUR MISSION

Precision Surveys, Elevated

"At SkyMap, our mission is to revolutionize surveying through advanced drone technology, delivering accurate, efficient, and innovative aerial mapping solutions for construction and industry. We are committed to providing precise data and actionable insights that empower industries to make informed decisions, while maintaining affordability."

Our Product

We plan to develop a precision flight controlled drone to accurate measure landscapes and buildings with centimeter level precision. In environments where manpower is limited, SkyMap drones will reduce costs and enable teams to streamline the construction process.
